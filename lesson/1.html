Italics

To make a phrase italic in Markdown, you can surround words with an asterisk (* ). For example, *this* word would become italic.

In the box below, make the word "not" italic.

Similarly, to make phrases bold in Markdown, you can surround words with two asterisks ( ** ). This will **really** get your point across.
